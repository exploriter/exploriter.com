It's not about what happens _in your sitting_, it's about what happens _from your sitting to your life_. Don't hold your practice hostage to the feeling of a clear mind. [^mindfulness]

1. <a href="/method/centering">Center</a> your posture, attention, and attitude.
2. Find your <a href="/method/rooting">root</a>.
3. Continuously return to centering your attitude and attention.

[^mindfulness]: As taught by <a href="https://johnvervaeke.com" target="_blank">Dr. John Vervaeke</a>.
