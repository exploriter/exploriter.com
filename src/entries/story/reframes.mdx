There are some _fundamental moves_ that when you can make them (and keep them), the world becomes a much bigger, brighter, and more coherent place. You become more _aligned with reality_.

1. ~I am an <u>individual</u>~ → I am a <u>person</u>.
1. ~We <u>have</u> relationships~ → We <u>are</u> relationships.
1. ~Faith is <u>belief without evidence</u>~ → Faith is <u>embodied and enacted belief</u>.
1. ~I'm feeling [lost|bored]~ → Seek novelty, make art, help out.
1. ~Buy & Sell~ → Kindly use/care for, fairly exchange, generously offer.
