Beauty is the perception of coherence. Where inner and outer, truth and goodness, mind and world, are aligned and in _right relationship_.

It is the most fundamental of the Goodness, Truth, and Beauty triad (transcendentals). Truth is its masculine, cold, outer expression, while goodness is its feminine, warm, inner expression. Beauty _informs_ truth, truth _serves_ goodness, and goodness _orients to_ beauty.

It is the guide by which story knows itself to be living, and the criterion by which we distinguish between the two fundamental expressions of story: _vision_ and _narrative_.

It is the attractor around which culture coheres and the evidence of the alignment and _right relationship between power and wisdom_.
