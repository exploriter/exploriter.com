**iatros** (<span className="text-olive-500">ἰατρός</span>): _physician_ or _healer_<br />
**-genes** (<span className="text-olive-500">-γενής</span>): _born of_, _produced by_, or _arising from_

Relating to illness (or death) <mark>caused by medical activity</mark> (rather than natural causes), including diagnosis, intervention, error, or negligence. Typically used in context of the overmedicalization, lack of personalized care, and holistic ignorance in modern industrialized medicine.
