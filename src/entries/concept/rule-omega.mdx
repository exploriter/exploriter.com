**Rule Omega**[^rule-omega] is a rule for generative conversations. This requires an upfront agreement between the participants to act in good faith and present with a generosity of spirit.

- Everyone has something worth listening to and engaging with
- Grant the benefit of the doubt
- Seek first to understand before disagreeing
- Ask questions

This is not a call to focus on the signal at the exclusion of the noise, but to lead with the intent to understand the signal before criticizing the noise if necessary.

[^rule-omega]: From a conversation between Jordan Hall, Daniel Schmachtenberger, and Jamie Wheal on <a href="https://youtu.be/rQA93ulyigg" target="_blank">Rebel Wisdom</a>.
