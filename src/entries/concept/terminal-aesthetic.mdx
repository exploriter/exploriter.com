**Terminal Aesthetic** is a modern "design" style by engineers who build interfaces that is characterized by functional minimalism, visual austerity, and an absence of emotional, sensory, and otherwise embodied intelligence. Logically sound but sterile and unmistakably lacking in experiential refinement.

The term plays on "terminal" in two senses:

1. **Computational** — evoking command-line terminals, where interaction is textual, flat, and unforgiving.
2. **Existential** — suggesting a kind of design that is fatal, lifeless, or hostile to organic experience.

---

**Promptcore**: a sub-style of the _terminal aesthetic_ characterized by direct visual or conceptual reference to command-line interfaces, prompts, or coding environments. Nostalgia for early computing mixed with a raw rejection of visual comfort.
