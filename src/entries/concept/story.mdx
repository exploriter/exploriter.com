**Story** is the _living mediator of the relationship between potentiality and actuality_. It is the means by which we explore, illustrate, and communicate complex ideas, values, and ultimately create the future. As the carrier of meaning and coherence, _story shapes how reality unfolds_.

When perceived and enacted through <a href="/concept/love">love</a>, it becomes <a href="/concept/vision">_vision_</a>—a sacred guide toward goodness and truth through beauty. When manipulated through <a href="/concept/fear">fear</a>, it becomes <a href="/concept/narrative">_narrative_</a>—a reductive control structure that masquerades as meaning.

While many stories arise in mundane or seemingly neutral forms, not obviously led by love or fear, to the degree they guide perception and action (ultimately choice), they lean toward one or the other of these two archetypal poles.
